What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the practice of optimizing your website so that it ranks higher in search engine results like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. The goal is to increase organic traffic (non-paid visits) to your website.

How to Use SEO?

  • Keyword Research: Find what your audience is searching for.
  • On-Page SEO: Optimize titles, headings, meta tags, and content.
  • Technical SEO: Improve website speed, mobile-friendliness, and crawlability.
  • Content Creation: Publish high-quality, useful, and relevant content.
  • Backlinks: Get links from other trusted websites to boost authority.
